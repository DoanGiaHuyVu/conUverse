Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a study group?

Navigate to the Study Groups page, click on "Create New Group," and fill in the required information about your group.

How can I change my privacy settings?

Go to Settings > Privacy Settings to adjust your profile visibility and messaging preferences.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the three dots (...) next to any post or comment and select "Report" to notify our moderation team.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days through the Settings > Account Settings page.
